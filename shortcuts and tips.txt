////////////////////////SHORTCUTS BLENDER///////////////////////////////////
G					Move
R					Rotate
I 					insert keyframe
X					delete
P					seperate
F					Fill
L					Link (usefull for selecting parts of mesh)
alt+I 				Clear the keyframe
alt+E 				extrude faces (along Normals)
shift+A 			Add
shift+D 			duplicate
cntr+r 				loop cut
cntr+b 				Bevel
shift+rightclick 	set cursor
